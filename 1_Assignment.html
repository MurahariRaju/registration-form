Rules for Students to secure certificate from SURE TRUST

  1. All classes are conducted online either on SURETRUST dashboard or on Google meet.
  2. Students are required to login for all classes regularly and punctually.
  3. Students are required to turn on cameras during the entire class during the entire course period. This rule is introduced to ensure that students are attending the classes sincerely which alone will help in skill upgradation in true sense.
  4. Students are required to follow modesty in dress policy for all classes, and for all common events in SURE TRUST.
  5. Students need to put in minimum 90% of attendance during the four months training period. If there is any shortfall in attendance, students need to repeat the course for getting the certificates.
  6. All the students must be in the class for the entire one-hour duration. At the time of taking attendance whoever is there only those names will be taken for that day's Attandance. Attandance will be taken anytime during this hour.
  7. SURE Trust does not give leave for your college exams of any kind because we have students from all over India and the course has to be completed in four months time. Only those who can manage their time for both need to join these courses.
  8. If a student is not able to join any class, he/she is expected to post the message in the course group and seek trainer’s permission. If anyone absents without prior intimation will lose admission for the course.
  9. The uniqueness of SURE TRUST training is to help students develop overall personality. To this extent SURE TRUST has introduced Life Skills Training (LST). On alternate SUNDAYs, LST sessions are conducted in the evenings between 6pm and 7pm, to facilitate students develop human values and other soft skills like communication, team spirit, professionalism in interaction with peers, superiors, and others; develop public speaking skills and improve the expression of thought professionally; and so on. All the students must attend 8 LST sessions during the four months training period. Only those students who attend all 8 LST sessions will be eligible for getting the course training completion certificate.
  10. During the four-month training of any course, students are required to complete the industry relevant mini projects/assignments given by the trainers on time using one’s own understanding. These projects will enable out of box thinking and give hands on experience in the skills that are being learnt.
  11. During the end of the course, all students are required to consolidate these projects/assignments into a course report and submit to the trainer. The trainer will sign the course report to authenticate that the course report represents the original work of the student. Two SURE TRUST members also will sign the report to testify that the course has been conducted as per the prescribed norms.
  12. Only when the students submit the course report and attend all the eight LST sessions besides attending the daily classes regularly and punctually, certificates will be issued.
  13. Students are required not to save any unwarranted pictures as the profile pictures in the mobiles or systems which get reflected while attending the classes.
  14. Students should not message trainers directly as this would amount to wasting of their precious time. Any subject related doubts or information needed should be posted only in the course groups.
  15. Students must understand that only with 100% focus, hard work and determination, one can achieve the success.
  16. Students! Please understand that not being serious in classes will amount to exploitation of freely available resources. Every course will go for 4 months duration without dropping midway.
  17. SURE Trust does not give leave for any kind of exams. Being prospective engineers and Managers, you all need to manage time for multitasking. Therefore, please manage your time for your exam preparation as well as attending classes in SURE Trust. Only those students who can manage time for both, need to join and continue the courses. I hope all the students will understand, cooperate, and successfully complete the course.

Awards and Recognition of Participation

  1. Special prizes will be given to students who put in 100% attendance by following all the rules and discipline, during the four-month training.
  2. Special prizes will be given for students who complete the projets with creativity and innovative ideas.
  3. Special Batch prizes will be given to that course group in which all the members will follows all the above rules as a group.
  4. Participation certificates will be given to students who participate in various activities launched as part of LST sessions.
